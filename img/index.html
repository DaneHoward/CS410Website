Welcome, Friend

The Never Alone app connects individuals at risk for suicide with the help they need when they need it most.

A Unique Solution

Never Alone monitors your condition and proactively sends notifications to friends and family

Proactive

Never Alone calls on your strongest support when you are feeling at your lowest.

Innovative

Never Alone offers a new line of protecion for individuals experiencing behavioral health issues

Connected

Never Alone will also call a trained professional if your support team would like to give you the best help they can..

The Problem

As it stands...

Every day over 121 people take their life throughout the world. While we may not know your personal story, we know what it is like to suffer with depression and have suicidal thoughts and we know how to help. We don't ask that you share your pain with someone you don't know or trust, we just want to help to give you one clear button to press when you feel like there is no other option.

Patient Driven

Suicidal patients must actively seek help to get it

Non-deterministic

A sufferer's support team must go for long periods of time without knowing their loved one's condition

Disconnected

Each part must act individually to monitor and repond to problems as they arise.
