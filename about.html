Robert J. Cramer, Ph.D Associate Professor
School of Community & Environmental Health
Old Dominion University

Robert J. Cramer, Ph.D. is an Associate Professor of Community Health at Old Dominion University (ODU). Before joining ODU in 2016, he served as faculty/staff at the University of Alabama Department of Criminal Justice, Sam Houston State University Department of Psychology, and University of California San Francisco Department of Psychiatry. His professional background includes a Ph.D. in clinical psychology, with psychology-law and statistics concentrations. As a broadly trained social scientist, Cramer’s research, teaching, and consulting applies psychological theory to social, legal and clinical-health issues. His areas of expertise include suicide prevention, violence, prejudice, LGBTQ health, and legal decision-making.
